Projects

WeChat mini program to solve the employment problem of disabled people: (project 1)
As a vulnerable group, the disabled have less social and work ability than ordinary people, and may have difficulty in traveling and low work ability. Moreover, HR in the company generally considers employees from the perspective of work efficiency, and most of the disabled can not meet their work needs. In fact, people with disabilities will adapt to their own incomplete parts, so that they can complete some tasks based on their existing abilities. At the same time, they also need to get paid for the work they can do, rather than just relying on the government and social assistance and welfare or the care of relatives, so that their subsequent lives become monotonous. They are fully capable of living a better life and even fulfilling their own value. But for people with disabilities, access to employment information is so limited that they are unable to find the jobs they can. There may be a way to break all this.
Capacity:
WeChat mini program has a powerful cloud database, enough to record the information of tens of thousands of disabled people.
Impact & Uniqueness:
Our WeChat mini program are the only now existing app for employment, they will have no pressure in find the job, since there are no discrimination.

Smart Voice Trash Can: (project 2)
In order to solve the difficulties of garbage classification, people are not familiar with different definitions of garbage and other obstacles to the implementation of garbage classification, the government will generally place some slogans in public places, provide some popular science videos on the Internet and provide public lectures and publicity activities to enhance people's awareness and knowledge of garbage classification. However, the effect of these publicity methods is limited, and many people turn a blind eye to it, and can not implement the garbage classification action. Moreover, garbage classification is very troublesome for many people, and many people do not classify even if they understand the importance of garbage classification, because of inconvenience and other reasons. What I think is that the current situation of the difficulty of garbage classification can be solved through the form of science and technology, so as to make garbage classification easier, so as to accelerate the implementation of garbage classification. The core of my project is to explore feasible solutions by understanding and investigating some problems commonly existing in garbage classification. On this basis, I use some technical means to solve the problem of garbage classification difficulties in view of people's difficulties in garbage classification.

Leader, President of Dormitory Student Union, Student Council:

Upon receiving survey results that clearly indicated a significant level of dissatisfaction among students with their dormitory living conditions, I recognized the need for proactive measures. To tackle this issue head-on, I spearheaded the establishment of a dedicated Dormitory Student Union.
The primary objective of this newly formed union was to enhance the overall quality of life for students residing in the dormitories, with a specific focus on improving their experiences during weekends.
This endeavor involved a range of responsibilities and activities: Survey and Feedback Collection: We initiated a comprehensive survey to delve deeper into the specific issues and concerns that students were facing. Collaborative Decision-Making: As the President of the Dormitory Student Union, I facilitated collaborative decision-making processes. We engaged with student representatives, residents, and other stakeholders to prioritize and address the most pressing concerns.

Advocacy and Communication: Advocacy played a significant role in our efforts. We communicated the concerns and needs of the dormitory residents to the relevant authorities within the university, ensuring that their voices were heard and considered in decision-making processes.
